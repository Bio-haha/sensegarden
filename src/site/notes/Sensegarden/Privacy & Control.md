#sensegarden 

- [[Sensegarden/Selective publishing\|Selective publishing]] - Only notes explicitly marked with dg-publish: true are published
- [[Sensegarden/No accidental leaks\|No accidental leaks]] - Linked notes are never auto-published; you decide what goes public
- [[Sensegarden/Full control\|Full control]] - Your private notes stay private until you choose to share them
